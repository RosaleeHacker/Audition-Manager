I wanted to provide you with some more information about this project.

I really enjoyed making this app. There was some additional functionality I wanted to implement within this app but I couldn't. I tried to make it so that you could add a show, then all the auditionees would be added behind that. I tried but after 6 hours of trying to make it work, I gave up and made this app primarily just for auditionees. I made the the auditionee design and implementation as close to my primary design as possible.

I want to keep trying to figure out the show thing if I have more time. With what I have, I learned so much about design, Angular and SQLite. I've spent over 12 hours on this app so far, and I'm pretty happy with the results. I hope you enjoy it!
